Serving Tray

Create a stylish and functional serving tray for breakfast in bed or entertaining guests.

Materials & Tools

  • 1x12 board (18–24")
  • 2 handles
  • Screws
  • Drill
  • Sandpaper
  • Stain or paint (optional)
  • Polyurethane (optional)
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut and sand the board.
  2. Attach handles near the short ends using screws.
  3. (Optional) Paint or stain and seal with polyurethane for durability.
