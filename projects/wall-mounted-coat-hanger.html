Wall-Mounted Coat Rack

Build a simple and functional coat rack to keep your entryway organized.

Materials & Tools

  • 1x6 board (36"–48")
  • 4–6 coat hooks
  • Screws for hooks & mounting
  • Drill, screwdriver
  • Sandpaper
  • Level
  • Wall anchors (if no studs)
  • Paint or stain (optional)
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut board to length.
  2. Sand edges and surface.
  3. Paint or stain (optional).
  4. Mark and drill holes for hooks, then attach them.
  5. Use a level to align board on the wall.
  6. Drill pilot holes into studs or use anchors.
  7. Mount rack with screws.
