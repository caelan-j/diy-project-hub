Mini Garden Tool Shed

Build a compact tool shed for storing your gardening essentials.

Materials & Tools

  • 6 × 2x4 @ 8 ft
  • 1 × T1-11 siding or 1/2" plywood
  • Hinges, handle, latch
  • Roofing shingles or tin
  • Circular saw, hammer, drill, square
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Frame base and walls from 2x4s.
  2. Attach siding and cut out door.
  3. Add sloped roof frame.
  4. Mount shingles or metal roof.
  5. Add shelf inside if desired.
