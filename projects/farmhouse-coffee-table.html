Farmhouse Coffee Table with Storage

Build a rustic farmhouse-style coffee table with a lower shelf for storage.

Materials & Tools

  • 4 × 2x4 @ 8 ft
  • 3 × 1x6 @ 8 ft
  • 1 × 2x2 @ 8 ft (optional cross support)
  • 1 sheet 1/2" plywood
  • Pocket hole jig, drill
  • Pocket hole screws, wood finish
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Build apron frame to fit top.
  2. Attach legs with pocket screws.
  3. Add lower shelf support.
  4. Secure plywood shelf.
  5. Attach top boards.
  6. Finish and optionally distress.
