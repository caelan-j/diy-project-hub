Simple Bookshelf (3-Tier)

Build a basic bookshelf with three shelves to organize books or display decor.

Materials & Tools

  • 3 shelves: 1x10" boards (24" long)
  • 2 side panels: 1x10" (30" long)
  • 1 back panel: ¼" plywood (24"x30")
  • 1½"–2" wood screws
  • Drill, screwdriver
  • Wood glue
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint/stain (optional)
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sand all pieces.
  2. Attach each shelf between side panels using glue and screws (pre-drill).
  3. Ensure it's square before tightening.
  4. Attach back panel using screws or nails.
  5. Fill screw holes and finish as desired.
