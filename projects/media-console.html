Media Console with Sliding Barn Doors

Build a functional media console with rustic barn doors that slide to reveal storage.

Materials & Tools

  • 2 × ¾" plywood sheets
  • 4 × 1x3 @ 8 ft
  • Barn door hardware kit
  • Screws, wood glue, paint or stain
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut and assemble the plywood carcass for the console.
  2. Face frame with 1x3 trim boards for a finished look.
  3. Install barn door track hardware above the console.
  4. Attach sliding doors with rollers.
  5. Paint, stain, or finish as desired.
