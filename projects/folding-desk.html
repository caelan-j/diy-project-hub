Wall-Mounted Folding Desk

Create a space-saving desk that folds up neatly when not in use.

Materials & Tools

  • 1 × 3/4" plywood sheet
  • 2 × 1x3 @ 8 ft
  • 1 × piano hinge (30–36")
  • Magnetic latch, chain or folding brackets
  • Drill, saw, level
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut plywood and frame edges with 1x3 trim.
  2. Attach piano hinge and mount cleat to wall.
  3. Install folding supports.
  4. Sand and paint.
