Coffee Table (Simple)

A sturdy and stylish coffee table built from 2x4s and 1x6s—great for beginners and perfect for any living room.

Materials & Tools

  • 2x4s for frame and legs
  • 1x6 or 1x8 boards for tabletop
  • Screws (2½")
  • Wood glue
  • Drill
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint or stain
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Build a rectangular frame using 2x4s.
  2. Attach legs to the inside corners of the frame with screws and glue.
  3. Attach top boards across the frame with screws from above or from underneath into supports.
  4. Sand and finish.
