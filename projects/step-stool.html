Step Stool

A sturdy step stool for reaching high shelves, built with basic cuts and minimal tools.

Materials & Tools

  • Top: 1x12 (24" long)
  • Legs: 2 angled side boards (from same 1x12)
  • Screws
  • Drill, saw, sander
  • Wood glue
  • Paint or stain (optional)
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut top and two side legs (use a triangle shape for legs or straight boards with angled ends).
  2. Sand edges.
  3. Attach legs to underside of top with screws and glue.
  4. Make sure it sits level before tightening.
  5. Finish as desired.
