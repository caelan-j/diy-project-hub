Planter Box

Build a rustic wooden planter box for your patio or garden using fence boards or cedar planks.

Materials & Tools

  • 5 fence boards or cedar planks
  • Screws (1½"–2")
  • Drill
  • Wood glue
  • Sandpaper
  • Outdoor stain/sealer (optional)
  • Landscape fabric (optional)
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut 2 long sides (e.g., 24"), 2 short sides (e.g., 12"), and a bottom board.
  2. Sand all pieces.
  3. Assemble box using glue and screws through side boards into ends of the front/back boards.
  4. Attach bottom board.
  5. Line inside with fabric (if for plants).
  6. Apply outdoor finish if desired.
