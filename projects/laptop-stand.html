Laptop Stand

Build an ergonomic wooden laptop stand to improve posture and airflow during work or study.

Materials & Tools

  • 1x12 board (~14" long)
  • 2 small 1x2 risers (~3–4" tall)
  • 2 front stops (~6" 1x2s)
  • Screws
  • Wood glue
  • Drill, sander
  • Optional paint/stain
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sand all pieces.
  2. Glue and screw risers underneath the board.
  3. Attach the small front stops at the edge to prevent slipping.
  4. Paint or finish if desired.
