Shoe Rack (2-Tier)

Build a compact two-tier shoe rack to keep your footwear neat and accessible.

Materials & Tools

  • 4 legs: 1x2s or 2x2s (18")
  • 4 long rails: 1x2s (~30")
  • 8–10 slats: 1x3s or 1x2s (~30")
  • Screws (1¼"–1½")
  • Wood glue
  • Drill, square, tape measure
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint or stain (optional)
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Build 2 rectangular shelf frames with long rails.
  2. Glue and screw slats across each frame evenly.
  3. Attach 4 legs by screwing through the frame into the leg ends.
  4. Check for square and level.
  5. Sand and finish as desired.
