Birdhouse

Build a cozy birdhouse to welcome feathered friends to your backyard.

Materials & Tools

  • 1x6 boards (~6 ft total)
  • Screws or nails
  • Drill
  • Hole saw/spade bit (1½")
  • Sandpaper
  • Hinges (optional)
  • Exterior-safe paint or stain
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut the following pieces:
    • Front & back: 9"x6"
    • Sides: 8" angled cuts
    • Bottom: 4"x4"
    • Roof: 7"x6" (2 pieces)
  2. Drill a 1½" entrance hole in the front panel.
  3. Sand all parts to smooth edges.
  4. Assemble box using wood glue and screws or nails.
  5. Drill small drainage holes in the bottom panel.
  6. Attach roof panels with overlap or add hinges for cleaning access.
  7. Paint or seal with exterior-safe finish (non-toxic for birds).
