Wooden Crate

A rustic wooden crate perfect for storage or decoration.

Materials & Tools

  • 6–8 slats (1x4 or pallet wood)
  • 2 side panels: 1x10 or 1x12 (12" tall)
  • Screws or nails
  • Drill/screwdriver or hammer
  • Wood glue
  • Sandpaper
  • Optional: rope or cut-in handles
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut slats and side panels to size.
  2. Sand all parts.
  3. Glue and screw or nail 3 slats onto each side.
  4. Add 2–3 slats to the bottom.
  5. Drill or cut handle holes.
  6. Paint or leave natural.
