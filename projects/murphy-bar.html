Murphy Bar

Build a fold-down Murphy bar that mounts to your wall and tucks away when not in use.

Materials & Tools

  • 1 sheet ¾" plywood
  • 1 piano hinge (30–36")
  • Chains, screws, latches
  • Drill
  • Level
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint or stain
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut plywood pieces for the box and front panel.
  2. Assemble the frame and attach to studs on the wall.
  3. Use piano hinge to attach the front panel.
  4. Install support chains to hold the panel level when open.
  5. Add internal shelving and finish as desired.
