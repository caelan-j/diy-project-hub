Outdoor Potting Bench

Create a sturdy and functional potting bench for your garden projects.

Materials & Tools

  • 5 × 2x4 @ 8 ft
  • 5 × 1x6 @ 8 ft
  • 2 × 1x2 @ 8 ft
  • 1 box 2.5" deck screws
  • 1 box 1.25" pocket hole screws
  • Outdoor glue, stain/sealer, sandpaper
  • Circular saw or miter saw
  • Drill/driver
  • Pocket hole jig
  • Sander, measuring tape, clamps, level
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Build sides: Attach (2) rails to (2) legs using pocket holes.
  2. Connect sides with front/back rails.
  3. Add middle stretchers.
  4. Install cleats and lay slats.
  5. Sand, then stain or paint.
  6. Optional: add hooks or back panel.
