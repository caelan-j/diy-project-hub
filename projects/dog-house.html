Dog House with Gable Roof

Build a classic gable-roofed dog house to keep your pup cozy outdoors.

Materials & Tools

  • 4 × 2x2 @ 8 ft
  • 2 sheets ½" plywood
  • Roofing felt or shingles
  • Drill, saw, nails or screws
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Build the base frame and floor using 2x2s and plywood.
  2. Frame and attach the walls and cut out a door opening.
  3. Build angled roof panels and attach to the top.
  4. Nail on shingles or roofing felt for protection.
  5. Optionally insulate or finish interior for comfort.
