Raised Garden Bed

Build a durable 4x8 raised garden bed using cedar planks and basic tools. Great for growing veggies and herbs at home!

Materials & Tools

  • 6x cedar 2x6 boards
  • Deck screws (2.5”)
  • Electric drill
  • Measuring tape
  • Optional: corner brackets
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut cedar boards to desired lengths (2x 8ft, 2x 4ft).
  2. Drill pilot holes in corners.
  3. Screw boards together to form a rectangle.
  4. Level ground where the bed will go.
  5. Place and fill with soil.
