Pergola Swing Stand

Build a sturdy pergola-style swing stand for your backyard oasis.

Materials & Tools

  • 4 × 4x4 posts @ 8 ft
  • 4 × 2x6 @ 10 ft (for beams and braces)
  • Swing hardware
  • Concrete, drill, level, post hole digger
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dig holes and set 4x4 posts in concrete.
  2. Bolt 2x6 beams across the top of the posts.
  3. Add diagonal 2x6 braces for stability.
  4. Install swing hardware and hang your swing.
