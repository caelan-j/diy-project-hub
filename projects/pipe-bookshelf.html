Wood & Pipe Bookshelf

Combine rustic wood and industrial pipe for a stylish wall-mounted bookshelf.

Materials & Tools

  • 4 × 1x12 boards (36–48" long)
  • Black steel pipes and fittings
  • Wall anchors and screws
  • Drill
  • Level
  • Sandpaper
  • Stain or sealant
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut and sand wood boards.
  2. Assemble black pipe sections to form brackets.
  3. Mount pipe brackets securely into studs.
  4. Place shelves onto brackets and level.
  5. Seal or stain the wood as desired.
