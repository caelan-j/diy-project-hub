Bookshelf with Adjustable Shelves

A classic plywood bookshelf with customizable shelf heights to fit any collection.

Materials & Tools

  • 2 × 3/4" plywood sheets (4x8 ft)
  • 1 × 1x2 @ 8 ft (face trim)
  • Shelf pins, wood glue, screws
  • Table saw or circular saw
  • Drill + shelf pin jig
  • Sander, clamps, square
🛒 View Cart on Home Depot

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut sides and use shelf pin jig to drill holes.
  2. Build box frame with top, bottom, and sides.
  3. Add face trim.
  4. Sand, then attach back panel (optional).
  5. Finish with paint or stain.
